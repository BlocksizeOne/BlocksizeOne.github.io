2. Data We Collect

2.1 Location Data (Optional)

  • - Purpose: Suggest destination currency based on your location
  • - Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • - Storage: Country name only, stored locally on your device
  • - You can deny this permission and manually select currencies

2.2 Photo Library Access (Optional)

  • - Purpose: Save generated wallpapers to your Photo Library
  • - Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR)
  • - We only ADD images; we do not read or scan existing photos
  • - You can deny this permission and use screenshots instead

2.3 User Preferences

  • - Purpose: Remember your settings (currencies, fonts, colors)
  • - Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR)
  • - Storage: Locally on your device only (iOS UserDefaults)
  • - Never transmitted to our servers

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • - Personal identifiers (name, email, phone)
  • - Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • - Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • - Advertising identifiers
  • - Your existing photos or files

4. Third-Party Services

Frankfurter.dev (Currency Rates)

  • - Data sent: Currency codes and amounts only
  • - No personal data transmitted
  • - Privacy Policy: https://www.frankfurter.app/

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS encrypted storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

6. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you:

  • - Delete the App
  • - Clear App data in iOS Settings
  • - Reset your device

7. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15-22)

You have the right to:

  • - Access your data (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • - Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

  • - Restrict processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • - Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • - Object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • - Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
